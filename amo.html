ABOUT AMO

The AMO, or Adventist Men Organisation, The Adventist Men Organisation (AMO) is a vital ministry within the Seventh-day Adventist Church, dedicated to nurturing the spiritual, social, and practical lives of men in the church and community. Established to empower men to live Christ-centered lives, AMO encourages spiritual growth, leadership, family responsibility, and community involvement. AMO provides a platform where men come together to study the Bible, engage in meaningful service, support one another, and address the unique challenges they face as spiritual leaders in their homes, churches, and society. Through seminars, workshops, outreach programs, and mentoring opportunities, AMO helps mold men who are strong in faith, firm in character, and compassionate in service. The organisation emphasizes values such as:

  • Integrity and godly character
  • Leadership in family and church
  • Service to the community
  • Evangelism and mission outreach
.

Whether it’s through health initiatives, vocational support, or church projects, AMO is committed to developing men who reflect the love and teachings of Jesus Christ in their daily lives. It plays a crucial role in the overall mission of the church — to prepare people for the soon return of Christ — by equipping men to be active disciples and faithful witnesses in the world.
