ABOUT AMO

The AWM, or Adventist Women Organisation, The Adventist Women Organisation (AWM) is a vital ministry within the Seventh-day Adventist Church, dedicated to nurturing the spiritual, social, and practical lives of women in the church and community. Established to empower women to live Christ-centered lives, AWM encourages spiritual growth, leadership, family responsibility, and community involvement. AWM provides a platform where women come together to study the Bible, engage in meaningful service, support one another, and address the unique challenges they face as spiritual leaders in their homes, churches, and society. Through seminars, workshops, outreach programs, and mentoring opportunities, AMO helps mold men who are strong in faith, firm in character, and compassionate in service. The organisation emphasizes values such as:

  • Integrity and godly character
  • Leadership in family and church
  • Service to the community
  • Evangelism and mission outreach
.

Whether it’s through health initiatives, vocational support, or church projects, AWM is committed to developing men who reflect the love and teachings of Jesus Christ in their daily lives. It plays a crucial role in the overall mission of the church — to prepare people for the soon return of Christ — by equipping women to be active disciples and faithful witnesses in the world.
